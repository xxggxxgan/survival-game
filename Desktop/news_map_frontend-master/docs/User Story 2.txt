User Story : Someone moves to a new location and are looking for things to do to get acquainted with the city. They want to
find things going on locally as well as what people are like/what trends are prevalent nearby. If possible, they'd also like
to have company for going to these social events.

- The app can provide them a set of information regarding what concerts and events are happening in a selectd location
by way of the Eventbrite API and Facebook events from the Facebook API. Along with showing these actual events going on,
the app will also leverage social media to find out what kinds of trends occur in the city with Twitter or such,
as well as any local issues or stories occuring by the Google News API. Along with showing if any Facebook friends live nearby,
all this information can be a great starting point to begin exploring the new area.
